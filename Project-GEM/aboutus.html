The Project GEM Team

Christie Cineus

Website Editor, Researcher, & Writer

This project is very personal for me. When I immigrated to the United States in 2019, I struggled with balancing my Haitian identity and culture while learning about American identity and culture. Slowly but surely I was fortunately able to assimilate, because I had a good support system, but there are people I know that were not so lucky. They continued to struggle and took even more time to fully integrate themselves into American society. With this website we hope to lessen the rate at which this continues, providing a helping hand for those that don't have one. Outside of this project, I like to volunteer in various organizations to help my community in any way I can.

Monica Coira

Website Editor, Researcher, & Writer

Hi, I am a junior at School for Advanced Studies- North. In my role as website editor and writer, I worked to gather data, pictures, articles, and firsthand stories about the problems immigrants, such as my parents, faced when adjusting to life in America. By developing the AI Chatbot, Project GEM helped me gain experience with Microsoft Azure, HTML, and CSS, as well as learn from and collaborate with my teammates. When I'm not coding, I also enjoy reading, playing chess, and math.
