Miami Itinerary

DAY 1

Morning: Start your day with a stroll along the iconic Miami Beach Boardwalk. The boardwalk is free to access and offers stunning views of the ocean and the city skyline.

Afternoon: Visit Little Havana, Miami's vibrant Cuban neighborhood. Explore the colorful streets, browse the local shops and markets, and sample some authentic Cuban food from a local restaurant.

Evening: Catch the sunset at South Pointe Park, a beautiful public park at the southernmost point of Miami Beach. The park is free to access and offers panoramic views of the ocean and the city.

DAY 2

Morning: Visit the Wynwood Walls, a public art space featuring colorful murals and graffiti art by local and international artists. The Wynwood Walls are free to access and open year-round.

Afternoon: Head to the Vizcaya Museum and Gardens, a stunning European-style villa and gardens located in Coconut Grove. Admission is $22 for adults, but you can explore the gardens for free.

Evening: Experience the vibrant nightlife of Miami's Little Haiti neighborhood. Check out the local bars and restaurants and catch some live music or dance performances.

Become a Part of This Experience

DAY 3

Morning: Visit the Miami Design District, a creative neighborhood filled with art galleries, luxury boutiques, and stylish cafes. While shopping and dining here can be pricey, you can still admire the stunning architecture and public art installations for free.

Afternoon: Explore the Oleta River State Park, a peaceful nature preserve located in North Miami Beach. Admission is $6 per vehicle, but you can enjoy hiking, kayaking, and swimming in the park's beautiful natural surroundings.

Evening: End your trip with a visit to the Historic Virginia Key Beach Park, a historic beach and park that was once one of the few places in Miami where African Americans could enjoy the beach during segregation. Admission is free, and you can swim, sunbathe, and enjoy the park's many amenities.

Pembroke Pines

DAY 1

Start your day with a visit to the C.B. Smith Park. This is a great spot for families with lots of activities including swimming, picnicking, and playgrounds.

After lunch, head to the Pembroke Pines Farmers Market. Here, you can purchase fresh produce, baked goods, and handmade crafts from local vendors.

End your day at the Pembroke Lakes Mall where you can find a variety of shops and restaurants.

Become a Part of This Experience

DAY 2

Spend the morning at the Chapel Trail Nature Preserve where you can go hiking or biking on the trails and spot local wildlife.

In the afternoon, head to the Pembroke Pines YMCA Family Center where you can swim or take part in group fitness classes.

End your day with a visit to the Silver Lakes Park, a great spot for a picnic or to catch a beautiful sunset.

Become a Part of This Experience

DAY 3

Start your day with a visit to the Young at Art Museum. This museum has interactive exhibits and workshops for kids of all ages.

After lunch, head to the Fletcher Art and Cultural Center. Here, you can explore art galleries and take part in workshops and classes.

End your day at the Cinema Paradiso. This independent movie theater shows classic and foreign films.
