Resources for Immigrants

What do you need help with?

Here are some resources that can help immigrants with job training, English classes, and cultural assimilation programs.

Job Training

There are many organizations that offer job training programs for immigrants. These programs can help you develop the skills you need to find a job and build a career in the US.

Learn More

English Classes

Learning English is essential for immigrants who want to integrate into American society. There are many classes and programs available to help you improve your English language skills.

Learn More

Cultural Assimilation Programs

Assimilating into American culture can be challenging for immigrants. Cultural assimilation programs can help you understand American customs, values, and traditions, and provide guidance on how to adapt to your new home.

Learn More

Legal Aid and Advocacy Groups

There are many organizations that provide legal assistance and advocacy for immigrants.

Learn More

United States Citizenship and Immigration Services (USCIS)

This is the agency responsible for overseeing immigration to the United States. They provide a range of services, including processing visa applications, naturalization, and more. .

Learn More

International Organization for Migration (IOM)

IOM is the leading intergovernmental organization in the field of migration. They provide a range of services and support to migrants, including assistance with resettlement, return and reintegration, and migrant protection.

Learn More
