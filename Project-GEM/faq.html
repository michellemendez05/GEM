We Got Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What resources does the website offer for Latin American and Caribbean immigrants?

Our website offers resources to help immigrants assimilate into American culture, including English as a second language classes, information on obtaining legal status, and access to community services.

2. How do I sign up for English as a second language classes?

You can sign up for our English as a second language classes by filling out a registration form on our website.

3. Are the English as a second language classes free?

Yes, all of our English as a second language classes are completely free.

4. Are the English as a second language classes available in different levels?

Yes, we offer English as a second language classes in different levels to cater to different language proficiency levels.

5. Can I get legal assistance from the website?

No, we do not provide legal assistance. However, we can provide information on where to find legal assistance.

6. Can I volunteer to help the immigrant community through the website?

Yes, we have information on how to volunteer with local organizations that help the immigrant community.

7. How can I get involved with the organization?

You can get involved with the organization by volunteering, donating, or attending our events.

8. Does the organization hold events for the immigrant community?

Yes, we hold events for the immigrant community throughout the year.

9. Are there any requirements to attend the workshops and events?

No, our workshops and events are open to all members of the immigrant community.
